Returns, Refunds & Cancellation Policy

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of the original purchase date. To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original packaging and condition. Please contact our customer support team to initiate the return process.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within a certain number of days.

Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of the purchase. Please contact us immediately if you wish to cancel an order. If the cancellation is made within the stipulated time frame, a full refund will be issued.

Payment Integration

We use a secure payment gateway to process transactions. Your payment information is encrypted and securely transmitted to ensure the highest level of protection. We do not store any sensitive payment information on our servers.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about our Returns, Refunds & Cancellation Policy or the payment integration process, please contact us at info@rdeditor.com.
